Project Overview

20 Questions, is a math game that offers 2 different modes, one with unlimited lives, and another with only 3 lives. From there the player is given the option for different difficulties (easy, medium, and hard) easy being only addition and subtraction of numbers between 0-10, medium offers questions increase the numbers for addition and subtraction to 0-100, and includes multiplication and division questions with 0-10, and finally hard mode ups the addition and subtraction to 0-1000 and the multiplication and division to 0-100.
